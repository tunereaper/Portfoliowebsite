Branchless Banking Implementation (BLB) Project

1. Why?

a. For Business:
  • Expand financial inclusion in underserved rural areas, aligning with organizational and national objectives.
  • Generate additional revenue streams through increased deposits, transactions, and utility payments.
  • Establish Siddhartha Bank as a leader in innovative banking solutions.
b. For Users:
  • Provide seamless access to financial services such as deposits, withdrawals, utility payments, and transfers in rural communities.
  • Reduce the need for costly and time-consuming travel to distant bank branches.
  • Empower users with better financial literacy and access to secure financial systems.

2. Who Are the Users?

a. Persona:
  • Rural Farmers: Accessing banking for crop sales and personal savings.
  • Small Business Owners: Handling deposits, withdrawals, and utility bill payments.
b. Problems We Are Solving:
  • Lack of banking access in rural areas.
  • Dependency on informal and unsecure financial services.
  • Low levels of financial literacy preventing users from leveraging financial tools.
c. How Do We Know These Problems Exist?
  • Gap analysis of existing financial services and infrastructure.
  • Surveys and feasibility studies in remote locations.
  • Interviews with community members and potential agents.

3. Solution

  • Deploy BLB services in remote locations across Nepal to expand financial inclusion and accessibility.
  • Provide seamless access to financial services such as deposits, withdrawals, utility payments, and transfers in rural communities.
  • Equip local agents with training and technology to operate banking services and provide essential financial assistance.
  • Promote financial literacy through targeted community programs, helping residents understand and utilize banking solutions effectively.

4. Product Flow (Details of the Feature/Product)

a. Customer Journey:

Customer visits BLB agent for service → Agent performs transactions via POS machine → Customer receives instant receipt confirmation via SMS.

b. Features:
  • New Account Opening
  • Deposit
  • Withdraw
  • Transfer
  • Inquiry
  • Statement
  • Utility Payment
